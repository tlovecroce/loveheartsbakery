Cakes & Cookies

Specialty cakes and cookies (vegan and gluten free available)

Two days notice recommended

The Chocoholica

Deep, dark rich chocolate cake filled with chocolate mousse and covered with decadent chocolate buttercream icing.

Death by Chocolate

Layers of chocolate brownie, crisp chocolate meringue, chocolate mousse, mocha mousse, rich smooth ganache and a bit more mousse.

A Little Fruity

Your choice of yellow or white cake filled with seasonal fruits and whipped cream, topped with whipped cream icing.

Carrot Cake

Moist layers with carrots, walnuts & raisins (with or without nuts/raisins), filled and iced with traditional cream cheese icing.

Lemonade Cake

Smooth lemony pound cake filled with lemon curd and topped with a thin lemon icing.

The Bostonian

Traditional Boston Crème Pie—truly a cake—with buttery layers filled with vanilla pastry cream and topped with chocolate ganache.

A Little Tiramisu

Espresso-soaked yellow cake with mascarpone and cocoa filling; add Kahlua or Sambucca for a grown-up twist.

Chocolate Guinness Cake

Four-layer cake with the essence of Guinness Stout beer, layered with deep chocolate ganache.

Famous Sweet Potato Cake

Very moist bundt cake, rich with spices and flavored with rum. Surprisingly delightful—everyone comes back for more.

Cheesecake

Traditional 9-inch creamy NY style cheesecake in a crisp graham cracker crust, with or without assorted fruit toppings.
